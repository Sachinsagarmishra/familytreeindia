About the Program

Transforming school
campuses into
greener spaces.

Partner with us to help transform government school campuses across Bihar into greener, cooler, and climate-resilient spaces through survival-first plantation and sustainable campus greening.

Implemented in partnership with the Education Department, Government of Bihar.

Explore Partnership Options

Partnership Options

Choose your level
of impact.

01

Adopt a School

Support greening within a single government school campus.

This can include:

  • 🌳 50–100 native and climate-adapted trees
  • 🔧 Plantation and on-ground supervision
  • 🎒 Student participation and stewardship
  • 🛡️ Tree guards and survival support
  • 📊 Monitoring and impact reporting
Get Started →
02

Adopt a Block / Cluster

Support implementation across multiple schools within a block or cluster.

This can include:

  • 🏫 Multi-school plantation rollout
  • 📋 Standardised implementation and monitoring
  • 📍 Survival tracking and reporting
  • 📄 CSR documentation support
  • 🤝 Local engagement and visibility opportunities
Get Started →
03

Adopt a District

Enable large-scale environmental impact across an entire district.

This can include:

  • 🗺️ District-wide plantation implementation
  • 🔄 Coordinated rollout across schools
  • 📡 Structured monitoring systems
  • 📊 Impact reporting and documentation
  • 🌍 Regional engagement and visibility support
Get Started →
